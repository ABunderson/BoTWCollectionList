BoTW Collection

The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild is an extremely popular game and has sold over 29 million copies worldwide.

It is full of monsters, weapons, food, and other materials. Collecting these materials can be a struggle, especially when collecting specific amounts for armor upgrades or quests. This website lets you make a list of items to collect with specific quantities to streamline the process.

It also has the full Hyrule Compendium if you are missing an item and want to know what it is and the general location to find it in.
